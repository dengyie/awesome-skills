# Minimal Fix Plan

## Purpose

Constrain implementation to one attributable root-cause fix.

## Required Inputs

Define:

- the root-cause hypothesis
- the fix layer
- why that layer is correct
- the smallest acceptable change
- what is intentionally out of scope for this loop

## Prohibited Shortcuts

Do not:

- bundle unrelated cleanup
- refactor broadly without necessity
- change multiple layers hoping one will work

## Pass Condition

The planned change maps directly to the established root cause and is small enough to verify clearly.
