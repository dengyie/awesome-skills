# Symptom Capture

## Purpose

Separate direct observations from guesses before any repair workflow starts.

## Required Inputs

Capture:

- the observed symptom
- the visible failure behavior
- the failing surface, route, command, or scenario
- whether the evidence is directly observed or user-reported

## Prohibited Shortcuts

Do not:

- present guesses as facts
- skip over the actual failure language
- jump straight to the fix before the symptom is clear

## Pass Condition

The current failure is described clearly enough to begin reproduction or failing-evidence collection.
