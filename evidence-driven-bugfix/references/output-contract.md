# Output Contract

## Purpose

Provide a stable final report shape.

## Required Sections

Always report:

- `Current state`
- `Symptom`
- `Failing evidence`
- `Root cause`
- `Fix applied`
- `Fresh verification`
- `Outcome`
- `If not fixed, next loop or blocker`

## Rules

- keep each section evidence-based
- distinguish direct evidence from inference
- do not imply completion unless the outcome is `Fixed`

## Pass Condition

The final report truthfully states the current state and includes enough evidence for a reviewer to understand why the workflow stopped or continued.
